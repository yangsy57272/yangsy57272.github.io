About Me

Who I am

I am an IB student at Shanghai Pinghe Bilingual School with a passion for language, logic, and leadership. My academic journey spans competitive debate, olympiad mathematics, chemistry, physics, and robotics — but at the heart of everything I do is a love for clear thinking and compelling communication.

As a debater, I have competed on regional and national stages under WSDA and TOC formats, earning the title of national champion and Outstanding Speaker awards at every level. I believe the ability to construct and challenge arguments is one of the most transferable skills a young person can develop.

My intellectual curiosity extends to mathematics and the sciences. I qualified for AIME through AMC 10A, earned a Bronze Medal in the Canadian Chemistry Olympiad, and placed in the top 4% globally in the HIMCM mathematical modeling contest.

Beyond the classroom, I serve as art director for the school newspaper, produce videos for school events, and represent my school in robotics — our FRC team won the 2025 Halic Regional Championship in Istanbul and the 2026 Shanghai Regional Championship.

Research

Independent Projects

01
Computer Vision

Facial Expression & Identity Recognition System (Dlib)

Independently designed and built a multimodal biometric system combining face identity verification with real-time emotion classification using Python and Dlib. The system performs facial feature extraction, identity matching, and expression recognition in a single pipeline — a novel combination not available in competing open-source tools. Includes a custom sample database, algorithm optimization across multiple test cycles, and a formal research paper documenting the methodology and results.

Python Dlib Computer Vision CTB Competition (2026) Generation AI Hackathon
02
Mathematical Modeling

Emergency Evacuation Strategy Optimization (HIMCM 2024 & 2025)

Two consecutive years of independent research for the High School Mathematical Contest in Modeling, culminating in a Finalist finish (Top 4% globally) in 2025. Research focused on multi-agent dynamic risk modeling and evacuation pathway optimization in complex building scenarios under fire and gas leak conditions. Applied Koschmieder Visibility Model, Gompertz mortality functions, and HPC-environment simulation. Produced full research papers and strategy flow diagrams.

Multi-agent Modeling Gompertz Functions HPC Simulation HIMCM Finalist Top 4%
03
Energy Engineering

TENG-Based Hybrid Wave Energy Harvesting Device (Conrad Challenge)

Designed and prototyped a composite wave energy collection device integrating triboelectric nanogenerators (TENG) and piezoelectric effects to convert ocean wave energy into usable electricity. Completed full SolidWorks mechanical structural design, hardware selection, 3D printing and laser-cut model assembly, partial software programming, and a commercial feasibility analysis. The project incorporated smart remote monitoring and intelligent control integration. Awarded Silver Award at the Conrad Challenge National Stage.

TENG SolidWorks 3D Printing Energy Harvesting Conrad Silver Award
